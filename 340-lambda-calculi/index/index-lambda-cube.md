# Lambda cube

Lambda cube
- 3 dimensions
  - origin: terms may depend on terms (λ→)
  - x-axis: terms may depend on types (polymorphism)
  - y-axis: types may depend on types (type ctors)
  - z-axis: types may depend on terms (dependent types)
- 8 vertices
  - λ→
  - λ2
  - λω
  - λῳ

  - λ2ῳ
  - λP
  - λ2P
  - ΛC
