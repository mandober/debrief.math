$$

n \in \mathbb{N}, \quad S(n) = n \cup \{n\}
$$

## Hyper-0
- Hyper-0 operation is the Successor function
- Successor, $$S(n)$$
- Hyper-0, $$h_0(n)$$
- $$S(0) = 1$$
- $$S(S(n)) = S^{\ \circ\ n}$$
