## CL

## CIC

## IL

## LNC
The law of non-contradiction.
`¬(p ⋀ ¬p)`; it is logically equivalent to the LEM: `¬(p ⋀ ¬p) = ¬p ⋁ p = p -> p`.

## LEM
The law of the excluded middle, *tertium non datur*
