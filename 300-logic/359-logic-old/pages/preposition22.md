# Preposition

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proposition

A proposition takes the form of a logical formula.

- logical formula
  - atoms (atomic formulas)
    - logical constants (`true` or `⟙`, `false` or `⟘`)
    - logical variables (`p`, `q`, `r`, …)
  - compound formulas
    - logical connectives (`¬`, `⋀`, `⋁`, `->`, …)
    - atoms

- logical constants
  - `true` or `⟙`
  - `false` or `⟘`

- logical variables
  - `p`, `q`, `r`, …

- logical connectives
  - `¬`
  - `⋀`
  - `⋁`
  - `⊕`
  - `⟹`, `⟶`, ⟵, ⟸
  - `⟺`, `⟷`
  - `≡`
  - `↑`
  - `↓`
  - etc.
