Latex notes:
- almost all nominal keywords may be negated with `\not` prefix
  - $$a \ge b$$ and $$a \not \ge b$$ (`\ge` and `\not \ge`)
- directional keywords may be reversed by reversing the keyword spelling
  - $$\in$$ and $$\ni$$ (`\in` and `\ni`)



## Logic connectives

- $$\lnot $$ negation
- $$\land $$ conjunction, ∧
- $$\lor  $$ disjunction, ∨
- $$\to   $$ implication, →
- ↔
- $$\equiv$$ iff ≡

Logical constants
- $$\top  $$ true
- $$\bot  $$ false

Negation, `¬`
- `p ∨ ¬p`
- *"p or not p"*
