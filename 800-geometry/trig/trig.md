## Notation styles

always valid | only if arg is simple
-------------|----------------------------------------
sin(x)       | sin x        = sin x      = sinx
sin²(x)      | sin² x       = sin² x     = sin²x
sin²(2x²)    | NA
sin(4x)      | NA
2xsin(x)     | 2x sin x     = 2x sin x    = 2xsinx
3x²sin(x)    | 3x² sin x    = 3x² sin x   = 3x²sinx
5x³sin(x)dx  | 5x³ sin x dx = 5x³ sin x dx = 5x³sinxdx
