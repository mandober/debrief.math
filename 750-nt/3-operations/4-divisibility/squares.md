# Squares

- sequence of squares alternates between {even, odd, even, odd, … }
- even squares can be expressed as `4a+1`
- odd squares can be expressed as `4a` = `4a+(n/2)²` = `4a+n²/4`

* even square: x even ⇒ x² even
