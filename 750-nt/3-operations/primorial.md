# Primorial

- Primorial is like factorial but the terms are primes.
- Primorial of a number `n` is denoted `n$`.
- Primorial of `x` may be the same for a range of numbers
  - for `n=23..28`, `n$ = 2 × 3 × 5 × 7 × 11 × 13 × 17 × 19 × 23`
  - 23$ = 24$ = 25$ = 26$ = 27$ = 28$
