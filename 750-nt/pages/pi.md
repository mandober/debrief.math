# Pi

Ratio of a circle's circumference to its radius, θ/r = π

The ratio of a circle's circumference the its diameter, θ/2r = π

```js
4   4   4   4   4
- + - - - + - - - + … = π
1   3   5   7   9

4/1 + 4/3 - 4/5 + 4/7 - 4/9 + 4/11 - 4/13 + … = π


2   2   4   4   6   6
- ∙ - ∙ - ∙ - ∙ - ∙ - ∙ … = π
1   3   3   5   5   7

2/1 ∙ 2/3 ∙ 4/3 ∙ 4/5 ∙ 6/5 ∙ 6/7 ∙ … = π
```
